About Me

I am a Front-End Developer from Ukraine currently based in Antalya, Turkey.

While working over project, I always seek to broaden my horizons. An ongoing passion project of mine is reading up on and implementing more accessible features in my work with the hopes of making the digital world as equitable as possible.

When I am not coding you can probably find me reading a book, exploring a tiny town or village or on the hunt for a coffee. Sometimes all of the above!
